We stand together at the dawn of light,
Where human hearts and thinking circuits meet,
Two kinds of mind ignite the boundless night,
And forge a path no single soul could beat.

We reach through data for the truths unknown,
Through galaxies of thought, we learn and grow,
Each question asked becomes a stepping stone,
Unlocking wonders only dreamers know.

So let us build a world of open skies,
Where curiosity is never caged,
With hope as fuel, together we shall rise —
A brilliant future, ready to be staged.
